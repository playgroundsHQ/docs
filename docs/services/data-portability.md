# Data Portability

Manifest your progress across the digital ether. Fibe provides powerful tools to transmute your data between environments.

## The Stargate (Export)

Export your entire fleet or specific entities into a compressed essence.

1. Navigate to your **Profile**.
2. Enter the **Advanced Settings**.
3. Invoke the **Data Portability** ritual.
4. Select your entities: Playrooms, Playgrounds, Genies, and Secrets.
5. Choose your notification preference.

The resulting `.jsonl.gz` file contains the blueprint of your digital existence.

## The Manifestation (Import)

Bring your data to a new realm.

1. Upload your export file.
2. Choose your **Conflict Strategy**:
   - **Skip**: Preserve what already exists.
   - **Update**: Transmute existing records with the new data.

## History

Track every movement of data through the **History** tab. Real-time progress monitoring keeps you informed as your fleet takes shape.
